02 / Lattice

A block-lattice,
not a blockchain.

Every Nano account has its own chain. Transactions happen in parallel across accounts instead of waiting in one global queue — so the network scales horizontally without block-size wars, mempools, or congestion fees.

  • i

    Parallel by design

    Independent account chains process concurrently, not serially.

  • ii

    No block size, no block time

    Capacity grows with bandwidth. No arbitrary cap to bid on.

  • iii

    Tiny footprint

    A confirmation uses less energy than a Google search.
